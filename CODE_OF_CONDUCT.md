# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Pumkin to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone —
regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our standards

Behavior that helps:

- Being respectful and constructive.
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get oriented.
- Accepting feedback gracefully, and giving it kindly.

Behavior that isn't acceptable:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory or demeaning comments.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying these standards and may remove,
edit, or reject contributions or comments that violate them, and may ban
contributors for behavior they judge inappropriate.

Report unacceptable behavior to **hi@pumkin.app**. Reports are handled discreetly.
